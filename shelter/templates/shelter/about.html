About PawHaven

Dedicated to finding loving homes for pets in need since 2016

Our Mission

PawHaven Pet Shelter is committed to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming abandoned and neglected animals. We believe every pet deserves a second chance at happiness in a loving forever home.

What We Do

  • Rescue abandoned and surrendered pets
  • Provide medical care and rehabilitation
  • Match pets with loving families
  • Educate the community about responsible pet ownership
  • Support pet owners to prevent surrenders

Our Values

Compassion, Dedication, and Community are at the heart of everything we do. We treat every animal with respect and care, working tirelessly to improve their lives.
