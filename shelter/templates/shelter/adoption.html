Adoption Process

Start your journey to finding a new family member

How to Adopt

1

Browse Our Pets

Visit our pets page to see all available animals. Each profile includes photos, personality traits, and important information.

2

Submit Application

Found your perfect match? Fill out our adoption application form. We'll review it within 24-48 hours.

3

Meet & Greet

Once approved, schedule a visit to meet your potential new family member in person at our shelter.

4

Take Them Home

Complete the adoption paperwork, pay the adoption fee, and welcome your new pet home!

Adoption Requirements

  • Must be 21 years of age or older
  • Valid photo ID required
  • If renting, landlord approval for pets is necessary
  • All household members should meet the pet
  • Veterinary reference (if you have/had pets previously)
